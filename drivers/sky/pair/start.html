Connect Your Intellivent Fan

Before we search for your fan, put it in pairing mode:
  1. Make sure the fan is powered on
  2. Locate the WiFi / pairing button on the fan unit
  3. Press and hold the button for 8 seconds
  4. Wait until the LED starts blinking
Important: The fan stays in pairing mode for about 60 seconds. Once the LED is blinking, press Next below to search for your fan.

After pairing, the app will automatically read the authentication code from the fan. You can find it later in the device settings.
