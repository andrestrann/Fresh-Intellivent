Fresh Intellivent SKY Homey App
==============================

Control your Fresh Intellivent SKY bathroom fan directly from Homey via Bluetooth.

Features:
---------
- Read temperature, humidity, and fan speed
- Control fan speed, boost mode, pause, airing, humidity, light, and VOC sensors
- Flow triggers, actions, and conditions for automation
- Notifications for fan status changes
- Secure authentication (auto-capture during pairing)

Pairing Instructions:
--------------------
1. Power on your Intellivent SKY fan
2. Hold the WiFi/pairing button for 8 seconds until the LED blinks
3. In Homey, add the fan and follow the pairing steps
4. The app will automatically capture the authentication code
